ABOUT ME

My name's Ariel Hanna Jeon. I'm a Korean-American, born in Arizona but raised in Oceanside.

I'm a junior graduating from RBHS in 2021. I am currently involved with the Key Club, Women in STEM, and NHS.

Outside of school I'm a pianist. I've been playing for 7 years and counting, and while I've always had a love-hate-tolerance relationship with it, I've found other ways to pass my time.

I like to read (surprise surprise) and my favorite genre is typically non-fiction. I'm not involved with any sports at the moment, but I've done archery and taekwondo in the past
