ABOUT ME

Trivial things about me that you're welcome to know.

GENERAL INFO

My name's Ariel Hanna Jeon. I'm a Korean-American (who can't fluently speak it but can understand enough to eavesdrop) who was born in Arizona and raised in Oceanside, CA. I enjoyed being close enough to the beach I could bike there, but I didn't find my move to Poway in 2014 to be anything to cry about.

As you can probably tell, I'm currently stuck with a black/white minimalistic aesthetic, mainly because I find that coordinating colors is a huge hassle

I'm a junior graduating from RBHS in 2021. I am currently involved with the Key Club, Women in STEM, and NHS. I am earnestly looking forward to senior year, but not really what comes after that. I don't have a clear image of what I want to do in the future, but I'm leaning towards the science/STEM field

HOBBIES

I like to read (surprise surprise) and my favorite genre is typically non-fiction. It's impossible for me to name a favorite book or series, but Sarah J. Maas and Rick Riordan and childhood favorite authors.

I'm not involved with any sports at the moment, but I've done archery and taekwondo in the past
