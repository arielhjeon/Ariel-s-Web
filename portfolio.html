SCRATCH

Multiplayer Ping Pong: the goal of the game is to reach 15 points before the other player does. Player 1 controls are the WASD keys, while Player 2 controls are the arrow keys.

MIT App Inventor

Catch! A game where items are falling and a sprite slides horizonally to catch them. In this case, your avatar is a dragon, and you have to do your best to catch the falling coins and dodge the falling mines. Lives are lost if a coin reaches the end of the screen or if the dragon comes in contact with a mine.

INTERACTIVE FICTION

Stranded: A story where you're stuck in a few dimensions away from home and have to find a way to get back. Going from a world full of minions, to one of pirates and mermaids, you have to find a way to avoid certain death.
